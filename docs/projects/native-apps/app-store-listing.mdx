## Register an App ID

### 1. Retrieve your Bundle ID from AppPresser

Login to AppPresser and find your Bundle ID in the **Dashboard**.

![AppPresser Dashboard](./img/retrieve-your-app-bundle-id.png)

### 2. Register a New Bundle ID with Apple

Register a new Bundle ID in [Certificates, Identifiers & Profiles](https://developer.apple.com/account/resources/identifiers/bundleId/add/) in your Developer Account. Use the Bundle ID from AppPresser, and be sure to select **Push Notifications** under **Capabilities**.

![Apple Developer Account](./img/set-bundle-id.png)

## Add a New App

### 1. Add a New App in App Store Connect.

Visit the [Apple App Store Connect](https://appstoreconnect.apple.com/apps) to register a new App using the Bundle ID you just created. Click the plus button in the top left to add a new app.

![Apple App Store Connect](./img/add-new-app.png)

### 2. Complete the App Information

![Apple App Store Connect](./img/new-app-info.png)

Complete the following fields:

- **Platforms:** iOS
- **Name:** The name of your app
- **Primary Language:** English (U.S.)
- **Bundle ID:** The Bundle ID you created in the previous step
- **SKU:** The SKU of your app (This can be anything you want it to be.)
- **User Access:** Full Access

The click **Create**.

## Prepare for Submission

### 1. Add Screenshots

Under **Previews & Screenshots** drag the screenshots you created in the previous guide into the **Screenshots** section.

### 2. Add Metadata

Complete the following fields:

- **Promotional Text:** A short description of the app that will be used in some promotional areas of the app store.
- **Description:** A description of your app.
- **Support URL:** The client's app page on their website.
- **Keywords:** 5 keywords that describe your app.
- **Copyright:** The copyright holder for the app. (The legal name of the church or organization.)

Click the **Save** button.

### 3. General App Information

You must select a primary category of *Lifestyle* for your app in **App Information > General Information**. Secondary category is optional.

You must set up Content Rights Information (No, it does not show third party...) in **App Information > General Information**.

Under the **Age Rating** section, click the **Set Age Rating** button.

![Apple App Store Connect](./img/set-age-rating.png)

Most of our clients should be **NONE** for everything.

![Apple App Store Connect](./img/set-age-rating-2.png)

It is not "Made for Kids" and not "Restricted to 17+"

![Apple App Store Connect](./img/set-age-rating-3.png)

Save your changes.
