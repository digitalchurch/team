# App Store Listing

Apple App Store Connect: [https://appstoreconnect.apple.com/](https://appstoreconnect.apple.com/)

Help: [https://docs.apppresser.com/article/634-create-app-store-listings](https://docs.apppresser.com/article/634-create-app-store-listings)

## Prepare for Submission

The following fields are required before submitting the app for review.

- You must provide copyright information for the app you are adding under **Prepare for Submission**.
- You must choose a build in **Prepare for Submission**.
- Upload the Screenshots that were generated in the previous guide.
- Support URL
- Keywords
- Description

## App Information

- You must select the level of frequency for each Apple content description in the Age Rating section in **App Information > General Information > Age Rating.**
   - Most Church Apps should be NO for everything. Age Recommendation will probably be 4+. It is not "Made for Kids" and not "Restricted to 17+"
- You must select a primary category of *Lifestyle* for your app in **App Information > General Information**.
- You must set up Content Rights Information (No, it does not show third party...) in **App Information > General Information**.

## Price & Availability

- You must choose a price tier of $0.00 in **Pricing & Availability**.
